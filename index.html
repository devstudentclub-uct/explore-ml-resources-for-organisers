EXPLORE ML


WHAT IS EXPLORE ML?

Explore Machine Learning (ML) is a Google-sponsored program for university students to get started with Machine Learning. The curriculum offers 2 tracks of machine learning content (Beginner and Intermediate) and relies on university student facilitators to train other students on campus. 

Explore ML and Google Developer Student Clubs are collaborating to help student grow their knowledge and develop their skills in Machine Learning.

WHY IS EXPLORE ML USEFUL?

The biggest motivation is the acquisition of knowledge skills that will be valuable for your future.

Students who participate in the program will additionally:

  • Acquire foundational machine learning knowledge.

  • Build a network of like-minded, passionate students excited about applications of ML.

I want to host an Explore ML event... What do I do?

How to create the Explore ML event in GDSC Event Platform

  • Locate your chapter from within the dashboard and select Events from the left sidebar.
  • Explore ML Beginner Workshops
    • Click New Event and select Explore ML Beginner - Virtual or  Explore ML Beginner - In person
  • Explore ML Intermediate Workshops
    • Click New Event and select Explore ML Intermediate - Virtual or  Explore ML Intermediate - In person
  • Fill out the appropriate fields:  
    • Title, Short Description, Event Description
      • Please mention the facilitator's name in the event description
    • Topics - select Machine Learning / AI
    • Schedule & Agenda
  • Start sharing and promoting your event through your community and social media
    • You can use this poster template to share on social media, use hashtag #DeveloperStudentClubs
    • Feel free to also start sharing your permalink if your event is virtual
  • Organizer team (lead, core team, event organizers) should keep track of all attendees that take part in the event
    • IMPORTANT: Please check in your members during the event to make sure we can distribute the certificates to them. We will send out certificates during your graduation (mid 2021). Please refer to this for details on how to check in your members during the event

If you have any questions about Explore ML, please reach out to explore-ml-program@google.com
